Follow these steps to install the Sudoku Journal app on your mobile device:

  • Open your Chrome browser.
  • Type in the search bar: https://dhymcun.github.io/sudoku-journal/
  • Click the three dots in the upper right corner of the browser.
  • Then click "Add to Home Screen."
  • Finally, install the Sudoku Journal.
